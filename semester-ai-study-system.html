Building a Semester-Long AI Study System

February 18, 2026

Random AI sessions feel productive but lack structure. A semester system creates measurable progress.

Weekly Structure

  • New topic retrieval session
  • Weakness identification
  • Spaced repetition schedule
  • End-of-week cumulative test

Monthly Adjustment

At the end of each month, AI analyzes trends and increases difficulty where needed.
