About

This is my CAH 205 newsletter about applied AI for studying and real-world productivity. I am not trying to make AI sound magical. I am trying to use it in ways that actually help.

Who I am

My name is Dawson Myers. I am a college student and I like building simple systems that make school less stressful. I care about results more than hype, so I focus on AI workflows that are repeatable: tutoring-style questions, feedback on my answers, and plans that keep me consistent without wasting time.

What I post here

  • Prompts that turn AI into a tutor instead of a summary machine
  • Study workflows built around practice questions and explanations
  • Productivity setups that break big assignments into steps and schedules
  • What worked, what did not, and what I improved each week
