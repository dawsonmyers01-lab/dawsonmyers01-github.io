AI study prompts that actually work

January 24, 2026

Summaries feel productive, but they do not always stick. I get better results when AI helps me practice, catch mistakes, and focus on weak spots.

Three prompts I am using

1) Tutor mode

Prompt: “I am studying [topic]. Ask me 8 questions one at a time. After each answer, explain what I got right or wrong and why.”

2) Make it harder

Prompt: “Give me a harder version of that question” or “Add a real-world scenario where this concept applies.”

3) Diagnose weak spots

Prompt: “Based on my answers, list the top 3 concepts I should review and give me a 10-minute plan.”

Why this works

This forces retrieval practice instead of passive reading. If I can answer questions and fix errors, I usually remember the material longer.
