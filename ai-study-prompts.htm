AI study prompts that actually help

January 25, 2026

When people say they “use AI to study,” they often mean they ask for a summary and stop there. That can feel productive, but it is easy to forget the information later. A better approach is to use AI to create practice and feedback.

Here are three prompts I plan to use this week:

1) “Quiz me like a tutor.”
Ask: “I am studying [topic]. Ask me 8 questions, one at a time. After each answer, tell me what I got right or wrong and why.”

2) “Make it harder.”
After a correct answer, ask: “Give me a harder version of that question” or “Add a real-world scenario where this concept applies.”

3) “Find my weak spots.”
Ask: “Based on my answers, list the top 3 concepts I should review and suggest a 10-minute plan.”

The main idea is simple: use AI to generate active recall practice, not just a recap. If the tool helps you test yourself and fix mistakes, you are more likely to remember the material.
