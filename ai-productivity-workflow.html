A simple AI workflow for daily productivity

January 24, 2026

Productivity tools can get complicated fast, so I am starting with one small workflow: planning my day in a way that is realistic. The goal is not to do more tasks. The goal is to finish the right tasks.

Here is the workflow:

Step 1: Brain dump (2 minutes).
I list everything on my mind: homework, errands, messages, and deadlines.

Step 2: Ask AI to prioritize (1 minute).
Prompt: “Here is my list. Ask 3 quick questions to clarify deadlines and effort. Then give me a plan with 3 priorities and a time-block schedule for today.”

Step 3: Make the plan smaller (1 minute).
Prompt: “Cut this plan down to the minimum that still counts as a successful day.”

I like this approach because it forces clarity (what matters today?) and it protects my time by keeping the plan short. If I keep using it, I will adjust the prompts until they match my actual schedule.
