AI study prompts that actually help

The easiest mistake is using AI only for summaries. Summaries can feel productive, but they do not always stick. I get better results when AI helps me practice and correct mistakes.

Three prompts I am using

1) Tutor mode (active recall)
Prompt: “I am studying [topic]. Ask me 8 questions one at a time. After each answer, explain what I got right or wrong and why.”

2) Make it harder (level up)
Prompt: “Give me a harder version of that question” or “Add a real-world scenario where this concept applies.”

3) Diagnose weak spots (targeted review)
Prompt: “Based on my answers, list the top 3 concepts I should review and give me a 10-minute plan.”

Why these work

They force me to retrieve information, not just read it. If I can answer questions and fix errors, I can usually remember the material longer.
