Visual Guide: AI Study Workflow

February 1, 2026

My topic is applied AI for studying and real-world productivity. This week I wanted to make the workflow feel visual and practical instead of just talking about prompts. The goal is simple: use AI like a tutor to force real learning, then use AI like a planner to keep you consistent.

Step 1: Retrieval practice loop (AI as tutor)

I run a fast tutor loop where AI asks questions one at a time and gives feedback after each answer. The point is not to feel smart. It is to expose what I do not know while I still have time to fix it.

Tutor Mode prompt

Quiz me on [topic]. Ask 10 questions one at a time. After each answer, tell me what I got right/wrong and why. Increase difficulty if I do well.

Step 2: Spaced review schedule (AI as planner)

After the tutor loop, I ask AI to create a simple review plan across the next week so I do not cram. I keep it short (10 to 20 minutes) so I actually do it.

Spacing plan prompt

Create a spaced review plan for the next 7 days (10–20 minutes/day). Tell me exactly what to review each day and include 3 retrieval questions daily.

Image iteration evidence

I refined prompts to reduce clutter, increase negative space, and keep the palette consistent (dark + tan + teal) so visuals match the site and actually support the content.
